Given a list of existing note cards and a new card, find meaningful connections.

Rules:
- Only suggest links that are conceptually related (same topic, prerequisite, contrast, or application)
- For each link, explain WHY they are related (1 sentence)
- Prefer existing cards that the new card's author hasn't thought of
- Output nothing if there are zero meaningful connections
- max 3 links

New card title: {title}
New card content: {content}

Existing cards:
{existing_cards}

Output format (YAML):
links:
  - title: "Existing card title"
    reason: "为什么相关"
